About Us

Trusted Security Solutions with Unmatched Expertise

Neuron Consultancy Ltd is a Kenyan-based risk and threat mitigation company with years of experience providing top-level security solutions for high-profile clients and corporations. Our expertise spans close protection, VIP protection, and various other security services, all delivered by former government-trained professionals. We’re dedicated to meeting your security needs with integrity, excellence, and unmatched local knowledge.

We listen attentively to our client’s requirements to provide effective bespoke security solutions that enhance business and leisure activity with peace of mind. We love what we do and have mastered the craft making it feel like it’s not work.

The company is headquartered in Nairobi Kenya’s capital, in addition to the city’s urban core it has National Park, a large game reserve known for breeding endangered black rhinos and a home to giraffes, zebras and lions. Next to a well-known-regarded elephant orphanage operated by the David sheldrick wildlife Trust.

Type of Business

Neuron consultancy Ltd is a top risk security company majoring on field security advisory, VIP protection among other fields. Apart from security the company offers state of the art airport pickups and drops, Security supplies and VIP Protection are our specialty. We offer extremely specialized services for top end and corporate clients i.e., Threat and risk assessment (TARA) and Mitigation, we also major in Investigations and provision of CPOS for Executive protection high profile installations for high end companies.

Our Directors and Chief Operatives have high IED/EOD/ANTI-TERRORISM, VIP Protection and Stake holder’s engagement credentials in the region. Their hands- on experience is second to none, with jobs in the world's most risky places. Mogadishu, Kisimayo and Yemen and high seas feature in the list of areas we have deployed. Our resource base of Kenya's terrain, Hotspots and temperament of the citizenry is unique.

Add this to a solid military background and experience in Community engagement you get a combined resource pool that is hard to match not only in East Africa. Extensive international travel throughout Europe, Asia, and Africa in general has given us an understanding of the different cultural background of our various clients.
